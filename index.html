How verification works

Every channel on our list is independently verified by a human analyst — never bulk-copied from anywhere.

  1. Volume forensics. Upload cadence vs. channel age — humans can't publish 6 produced videos a day, every day.
  2. Template detection. Recycled scripts, title patterns, identical structures with swapped nouns.
  3. Synthetic markers. TTS narration fingerprints, AI-image artifacts, zero behind-the-scenes human presence.
  4. Evidence file. Each listed channel carries documented reasons — and a public correction process if we get one wrong.
We deliberately do not flag channels that merely use AI tools in a human-led workflow. The list targets mass-produced synthetic content farms — the ones your customers never wanted to be seen next to.
